# Score

The **Score** tracks the number of **[Enemy](Enemy.md)** objects destroyed by the **[Ship](Ship.md)** during a gameplay session.

## Requirements

1. The **Score** shall increment by 1 for each **[Enemy](Enemy.md)** destroyed by a **[Bullet](Bullet.md)**.
2. The **Score** shall be displayed in the top-right corner of the screen at all times during gameplay.
